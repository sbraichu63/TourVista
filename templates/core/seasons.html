India's Four Travel Seasons

India's diverse geography means different regions peak in different seasons. Here's your complete guide.

❄️

Winter Peak Season

October – February

The best time to visit most of India. Pleasant temperatures, clear skies, major festivals, and peak tourist activity.

Best Destinations
🏰 Rajasthan🏖️ Goa🕌 Agra (Taj Mahal) 🌺 Kerala🐘 Karnataka🌊 Andaman
Avg Temp: 12–25°C
Festivals: Diwali, Christmas, Pushkar Fair, Rann Utsav
☀️

Summer — Hill Stations

March – June

Hot in plains, perfect for hills! Escape to Himalayas, Western Ghats hill stations. Adventure season in mountains.

Best Destinations
🏔️ Manali🌲 Shimla🍵 Darjeeling 🌿 Ooty☕ Coorg⛷️ Gulmarg
Avg Temp: Hills 10–20°C, Plains 35–45°C
Festivals: Holi, Baisakhi, Summer Treks
🌧️

Monsoon — Lush & Green

July – September

India transforms into a lush green paradise. Perfect for budget travel, waterfalls, and Kerala / Northeast.

Best Destinations
🌴 Kerala🌺 Coorg💧 Meghalaya 🌿 Assam🏔️ Ladakh (Rain Shadow)🏰 Mysore
Avg Temp: 24–32°C
Festivals: Onam, Raksha Bandhan, Ganesh Chaturthi
🌅

Post-Monsoon — Festive

September – October

Transition season with clear skies, greenery, festivals, and comfortable temperatures everywhere in India.

Best Destinations
🐅 Jim Corbett🦁 Ranthambore🧘 Rishikesh ❄️ Kashmir🏖️ Goa (Starting)🌄 Sikkim
Avg Temp: 18–28°C
Festivals: Navratri, Dussehra, Diwali
